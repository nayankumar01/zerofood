These are the fallowing TIPS to save food

  • Plan your meals ahead of time to avoid overbuying .
  • Buy only what you need
  • Compost food scraps
  • Store your food properly to make it last longer.
  • Use leftovers to create new meals.
  • Donate excess food to local food banks or shelters.
  • submit expired food to factory to source Reduction
